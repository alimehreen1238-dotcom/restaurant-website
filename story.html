Our Story

A family's love for food, transformed into Karachi's most beloved dining destination

"Every dish is a letter
to my mother."
Founder's Letter

Born from a
Family Kitchen

It started with my mother's kitchen in Lyari — where the smell of nihari simmering overnight was our alarm clock. Every Sunday, our family would gather around a dastarkhwan spread across the floor, and food was never just food. It was love, memory, and identity.

In 2012, I opened Mehreen Restaurant not to run a business, but to share those memories with Karachi. To give every guest the feeling of being welcomed into a home where the cook pours her heart into every pot.

Today, twelve years later, that spirit lives in every dish our chefs prepare.

— Mehreen Ali, Founder

Our Journey

12 Years of Excellence

2012

The First Table

Mehreen Restaurant opens with 12 tables in Clifton. A 3-hour queue forms on the opening night. The Nihari sells out within two hours.

First Award

Pakistan Food Awards names us "Best Traditional Restaurant" in Karachi — just 18 months after opening.

2014
2017

Expansion

We expand to our current 80-seat main dining hall and introduce private event suites, serving over 1,000 guests per week.

Home Delivery Launch

We introduce our signature insulated packaging to bring the Mehreen experience to homes across Karachi.

2020
2024

Today

4,800+ monthly guests, 85 signature dishes, 18 industry awards — and the same family recipes that started it all.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

01

Authenticity

We never compromise on our original recipes. Every dish is made the slow, traditional way — no shortcuts, no substitutes.

02

Hospitality

In Pakistan, a guest is a gift. We treat every person who walks through our doors as family — with warmth, care, and attentiveness.

03

Quality

From sourcing to plating, we hold ourselves to the highest standards. We taste every dish before it leaves the kitchen.

04

Community

We employ local talent, source from local farms, and invest in the Karachi community that has given us everything.
